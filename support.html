Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I cannot sign in with Google.

Check your internet connection and ensure Google Play Services is updated.


Q: My learning progress is missing.

Sign in using the same Google account previously used.


Q: Ads are not loading.

Ads require an active internet connection and availability may vary by region.


Q: App is running slowly.

Update to the latest version and restart the application.
